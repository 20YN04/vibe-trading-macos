Build a Constrained Portfolio Optimizer for crypto.

Steps:
1. Fetch 2 years of daily OHLCV for BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, ADA, DOT, AVAX, MATIC (ccxt)
2. Calculate annualized returns, covariance matrix
3. Run mean-variance optimization (maximize Sharpe ratio) with constraints:
   - Max 40% in any single asset
   - Max 60% total in top 2 assets  
   - Min 5% in any asset
   - Max 200% turnover per rebalance
4. Compare 3 rebalancing frequencies: daily, weekly, monthly
5. Output: optimal weights per period, efficient frontier description, backtest metrics
6. Show: does constrained optimization beat equal-weight? BTC-only?

Goal: Optimized portfolio Sharpe > equal-weight Sharpe + 0.2.
