Build a Risk Management System for a crypto portfolio.

Steps:
1. Use the combined portfolio from factor-model.txt (top 20 crypto, multi-factor)
2. Calculate Value-at-Risk (VaR): historical simulation at 95% and 99% confidence
3. Calculate Conditional VaR (CVaR / Expected Shortfall)
4. Stress test: simulate 2022 bear market (-65% BTC drawdown), 2020 COVID crash
5. Compute: max drawdown, longest drawdown period, Calmar ratio
6. Output: VaR table, stress test P&L scenarios, risk metrics dashboard
7. Suggest position sizing: Kelly criterion, equal risk contribution

Goal: 99% daily VaR < 5% of portfolio, stress test max loss < 50%.
