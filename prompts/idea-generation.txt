Research Autopilot: hypothesis-driven alpha discovery.

Start with a hypothesis and let the agent run the full research loop.

Example hypotheses:
- "Momentum works better on large-cap crypto than small-cap"
- "Low-volatility crypto outperform during bear markets"
- "Volume precedes price: volume spikes predict 5-day returns"
- "Cross-sectional mean reversion is stronger on weekends"

The agent will:
1. Formalize the hypothesis with testable criteria
2. Load relevant market data
3. Generate a signal engine
4. Backtest the strategy
5. Report: IC, Sharpe, drawdown, regime analysis
6. Update the hypothesis based on results (reject, refine, or accept)
7. Output: hypothesis registry entry + run card

Goal: validate or reject 1 hypothesis per session.
