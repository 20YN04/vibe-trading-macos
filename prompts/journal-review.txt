Trade journal shadow account analysis.

Upload your broker export CSV. The agent will:

1. Extract your trading rules from historical trades
2. Build a Shadow Account: what would a rule-based system have done?
3. Diagnose behavioral biases:
   - Disposition effect: selling winners too early, holding losers too long
   - Overtrading: excessive turnover hurting returns
   - Anchoring: holding positions past rational exit points
   - Chasing momentum: buying after large moves
4. Compare: your actual P&L vs Shadow Account P&L
5. Output: bias report, missed opportunity count, counterfactual P&L

Goal: identify 1-2 concrete behavioral improvements for your next 10 trades.
