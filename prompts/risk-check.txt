Portfolio risk assessment.

Given the portfolio from factor-model results (or any portfolio):

1. Calculate 95% and 99% daily VaR via historical simulation
2. Calculate Conditional VaR (Expected Shortfall)
3. Stress test: simulate -50% crash scenario, -70% bear market
4. Compute: max drawdown, longest underwater period, Calmar ratio
5. Run Monte Carlo simulation (1000 paths, 1-year horizon)
6. Output: risk dashboard with all metrics, stress test P&L, MC distribution summary

Goal: 99% VaR < 5%, stress test max loss < 50%.
